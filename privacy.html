1. Who we are

B2B Better is an owned media agency registered in England & Wales. When this policy mentions "we" or "us," we mean the registered entity behind the brand.

2. What we collect

Name, work email, company, and anything you voluntarily send us via a form or email. We also collect basic analytics from visits — country, device, pages viewed. No ad-tech retargeting pixels live on this site.

3. Why we use it

To reply when you contact us, to send the occasional newsletter if you asked for it, and to understand which pages do their job. Nothing more.

4. Who we share with

A small number of service providers — email (Google Workspace), analytics (privacy-forward, cookie-free), and our CRM. All under a DPA. We don't sell data. Ever.

5. Your rights

You can ask us what we hold on you, correct it, or have it deleted. Email privacy@b2b-better.com and we'll turn it around within 30 days, typically much sooner.

6. Cookies

Strictly necessary cookies only. No pre-ticked marketing boxes, no dark patterns.

7. Contact

Email privacy@b2b-better.com. We'll reply within a working week.
